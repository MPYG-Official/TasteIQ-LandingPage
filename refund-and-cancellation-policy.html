Cancellation and Refund

Refund and Cancellation Policy

This refund and cancellation policy outlines how you can cancel your subscription or seek a refund for TasteIQ's services. Under this policy:

Cancellations

  • Subscription cancellations will be processed immediately upon request
  • You will retain access to the service until the end of your current billing period
  • No partial refunds will be issued for unused portions of your billing period
  • Any cancellation request must be submitted through your account dashboard or by contacting our customer support team

Refunds

  • Refund requests will only be considered within the first 7 days of your initial subscription (trial period)
  • To be eligible for a refund:
    • The request must be made within the first 7 days of service activation
    • Your account must be in good standing
    • You must provide a valid reason for the refund request

Service Issues

  • If you experience technical issues or service disruptions, please report them to our customer service team immediately
  • Our team will investigate and address your concerns within 1 business day
  • Service-related issues should be reported within 24 hours of occurrence for prompt resolution
  • If service issues persist and cannot be resolved to your satisfaction within the first 7 days of your subscription, you may be eligible for a refund

Refund Processing

  • Approved refund will be processed within 5 to 7 business days in customers account
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the subscription
  • Processing time may vary depending on your payment provider or banking institution

Non-Refundable Services

  • Enterprise-level customizations
  • Implementation and setup fees
  • Training services that have been delivered
  • Any subscription periods beyond the initial 7-day trial period

Service Termination

TasteIQ reserves the right to terminate or suspend service access:

  • In cases of violation of our terms of service
  • For accounts with pending or failed payments
  • For accounts engaged in fraudulent activities
  • When required by law or regulation

For any questions about this policy or to request a cancellation/refund, please contact our customer support team through your account dashboard

Note: This policy may be updated from time to time, and any changes will be communicated to our users via email or through our platform.
